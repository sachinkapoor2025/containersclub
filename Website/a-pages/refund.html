Refund & Cancellation Policy

This policy explains how refunds, cancellations, and subscription changes are handled across the Containers Club platform, including container listings, rentals, services, and premium tracking alerts.

1. General Policy Overview

Containers Club operates as a digital marketplace and service provider. Refunds and cancellations depend on the type of product or service purchased. All policies comply with applicable consumer protection laws.

2. Container Rentals

  • Rental payments already processed cannot be refunded once the container has been dispatched for delivery.
  • If a booking is cancelled before dispatch, a refund may be issued after deducting administrative and processing fees.
  • Delivery, pickup, and handling charges are non-refundable.
  • Security deposits (if applicable) are refundable after inspection of the container.

3. Container Sales (Buy/Sell Listings)

  • Containers Club does not process payments for container sales directly between buyers and sellers.
  • All sales transactions, refunds, disputes, and cancellations are handled by the respective seller.
  • We do not guarantee product quality, delivery timeline, or seller commitments.

4. Paid Tracking Alerts (SMS & Email Notifications)

  • Payments for tracking alerts are non-refundable once the service has been activated.
  • If you are charged but the service does not activate within 24 hours, you may request a manual review.
  • Cancellation of the alert subscription will stop future billings (for recurring plans).

5. Memberships & Subscription Services

  • Subscriptions may be cancelled anytime from your account settings.
  • Payments already made are non-refundable.
  • Your access remains active until the end of the billing period.

6. Incorrect or Duplicate Payments

  • If you believe you were incorrectly charged, please contact support within 7 days.
  • Refunds for incorrect or duplicate payments will be processed after verification.

7. How to Request a Refund or Cancel a Service

You may contact our support team using the following methods:

  • Email: support@containersclub.com
  • Subject: "Refund Request" or "Cancel Service"
  • Include: Your name, phone number, transaction ID, and description of the issue.
