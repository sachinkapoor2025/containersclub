Track Your Shipping Container Online in the USA

Tracking your shipping container in the USA has never been easier. With our advanced online container tracking system, you can instantly monitor shipment status, current container location, expected delivery timelines, and carrier updates—all in one place. Whether you're managing a single shipment or handling multiple containers across U.S. ports, our platform ensures real-time visibility and accuracy. Designed for shippers, freight forwarders, logistics companies, and individuals, this tool helps streamline your supply chain and reduce delays by offering fast and reliable tracking information. Stay informed at every step of the journey with comprehensive tracking features tailored for the U.S. market.

Enter Your Container Number for Real-Time Tracking

To begin tracking, simply enter your container number into the search bar. Our system immediately scans carrier databases to provide the most up-to-date information, including container movement, gate-in and gate-out records, vessel loading details, and port-handling updates. Real-time tracking helps you stay ahead of schedule, anticipate delays, and plan logistics more efficiently. This ensures seamless coordination whether your container is shipping domestically or arriving from an international route into a U.S. port.

How to Track Your Shipping Container Across U.S. Ports

Our tracking system is fully compatible with all major U.S. ports, including Los Angeles, Long Beach, Houston, New York–New Jersey, Savannah, Miami, and more. When you enter your container number, the platform automatically identifies the container's last recorded location and expected next movement. You can easily view carrier updates, vessel schedules, customs clearance progress, and drayage status. Designed for clarity and simplicity, the platform helps you monitor shipments with just a few clicks, eliminating the need to manually check multiple carrier websites.

View Live Container Status, Location & Delivery Updates

Once your tracking details load, you can access live container updates including current GPS-based location (if supported), port arrival confirmation, vessel berthing times, unloading updates, transit progress, and estimated delivery dates. This transparency ensures full control over your logistics operations. Whether your shipment is traveling by ocean, rail, or road within the United States, our tracking dashboard consolidates all updates in real time, helping you make informed decisions and reduce unexpected shipment disruptions.

Submit Container Number and Carrier Details

For accurate results, always submit the correct container number along with the shipping line associated with your shipment. This ensures our system retrieves the most relevant tracking data from the corresponding carrier. Supported carriers include Maersk, MSC, CMA CGM, Hapag-Lloyd, COSCO, Evergreen, ONE, ZIM, and many more. Providing accurate details helps speed up the tracking process and improves data precision.

Add Booking Reference or Bill of Lading (BOL) Information

In cases where additional verification is required, adding your booking reference or Bill of Lading (BOL) number can help refine tracking results. This option is especially useful for high-volume shipments, export-import documentation, and multi-container bookings. Including these details allows our platform to connect shipment data more efficiently, ensuring that all relevant container updates are captured.

Check Estimated Arrival Date & Current U.S. Port Location

The final section of your container tracking results displays the estimated arrival date, current port location, and upcoming transit milestones. This information helps businesses plan trucking schedules, warehouse availability, customs clearance, and final delivery operations. Whether your shipment is inbound or outbound, knowing the ETA and physical location of your container is essential for timely and well-coordinated logistics.
