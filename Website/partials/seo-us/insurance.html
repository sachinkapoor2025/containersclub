Container Insurance Solutions in the United States

Containers Club is developing container insurance programs designed for container owners, fleet operators, depots, terminals and logistics companies operating across the United States.

Why Container Insurance Matters in US Logistics

Containers in US supply chains move through ports, rail ramps, inland depots and distribution hubs, exposing assets to operational, transit and storage risks. Dedicated container insurance helps mitigate losses and maintain operational continuity.

Types of Container Insurance Coverage

Typical coverage includes physical damage, theft and loss, transit risks across road and rail, and weather-related incidents. Containers Club will partner with licensed US insurance providers to offer compliant coverage options.

Key Considerations for US Container Owners

Operators often evaluate coverage limits, deductibles, container age criteria, claims processes and exclusions. Containers Club will provide transparent policy details and guidance when insurance services go live.
